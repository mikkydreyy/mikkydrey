TOP 3 AFROBEATS ARTIST IN NIGERIA

  1. WIZKID

    2. Ayodeji Ibrahim Balogun born on July 16, 1990, is a prominent Nigerian singer and songwriter. Known for his smooth blend of Afrobeats, reggae, and modern pop, he has established himself as one of Africa’s most influential artists. His career began in earnest when he signed with Empire Mates Entertainment (EME) in 2009 and released his debut album “Superstar” in 2011. The album, which featured the hit single “Holla at Your Boy,” propelled him to fame in Nigeria and across Africa.

  2. DAVIDO

    3. Davido, whose real name is David Adedeji Adeleke, was born on November 21, 1992, in Atlanta, Georgia, USA. He is a Nigerian-American singer, songwriter, and record producer. Known for his energetic performances and fusion of Afrobeats with global pop sounds, Davido has become one of Africa’s most successful and influential artists.

  3. BURNA BOY

    4. Damini Ebunoluwa Ogulu born on July 2, 1991, in Port Harcourt, Nigeria, is a Grammy Award-winning Nigerian singer, songwriter, and performer. He hails from a family with a strong artistic and academic background. His grandfather, Benson Idonije, once managed the legendary Nigerian musician Fela Kuti, and his mother, Bose Ogulu, who later became his manager, is a renowned music promoter. Burna Boy’s father worked in the welding industry. He attended Montessori International School in Port Harcourt before moving to Lagos for his secondary education at Corona Secondary School. He later moved to London to further his education, studying media technology at the University of Sussex and media communications and culture at Oxford Brookes University.
